HIRIN - AI Features

Overview

HIRIN integrates Artificial Intelligence to improve the recruitment process.

ATS Resume Evaluation

The AI analyzes resumes and provides ATS compatibility feedback.

Resume Analysis

HIRIN identifies:

- Missing keywords
- Resume strengths
- Areas for improvement

Cover Letter Generator

Users upload:

- Resume
- Job Description

HIRIN generates a customized professional cover letter.

Cold Email Generator

The AI creates personalized cold emails suitable for job applications.

Interview Preparation

AI generates interview questions based on:

- Resume
- Job Description

AI Mock Interview

Provides:

- Personalized interview questions
- Performance evaluation
- AI-generated feedback

Job Recommendations

HIRIN recommends suitable jobs based on:

- Skills
- Education
- Resume
- Experience

Benefits

The AI features help users improve their resumes, prepare for interviews, and apply more effectively.
