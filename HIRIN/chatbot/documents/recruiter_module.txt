HIRIN - Recruiter Module

Dashboard

The recruiter dashboard provides hiring statistics including:

- Active Jobs
- Total Applicants
- New Applicants

Recent Job Postings

Recruiters can quickly view recently posted jobs.

Job Management

Recruiters can:

- Create New Job Posting
- View Job Details
- Close Job
- Repost Job
- Monitor Job Status

Job Status

Available statuses include:

- Active
- Closing Soon
- Draft
- Closed

Job Details

Each posting contains:

- Job Title
- Employment Type
- Location
- Posting Date
- Closing Date
- Job Description
- Required Skills

Close Job

Closing a job stops new applications while preserving previously received applications.

Repost Job

Recruiters can reopen a closed job by selecting a new closing date.

Candidate Management

Recruiters can:

- View applicants
- Review applications
- Monitor recruitment progress

Search

Recruiters can search using:

- Job Title
- Candidate Name
- Skills

Purpose

The Recruiter Module simplifies hiring and applicant management.
